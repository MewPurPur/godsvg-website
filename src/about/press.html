GodSVG naming

The name GodSVG should be capitalized correctly (S, V, and both G's as capital letters) when used in branding context. The name is a word play on Godot Engine (the tool used to create GodSVG) and the format SVG.

Do not use the logo in your own public projects in a way that implies endorsement from GodSVG developers.
